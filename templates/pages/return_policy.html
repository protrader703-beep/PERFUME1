Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Returns

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the product is unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as received.

To initiate a return, please contact us at support@attarstore.com with your Order ID and reason for return.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened or used Attar bottles
  • Items purchased on sale or discount
  • Gift sets that have been opened

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 5–7 business days via the original UPI ID used for payment.

Damaged or Wrong Items

If you received a damaged or wrong product, please email us within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost.

Questions? Contact our support team.
